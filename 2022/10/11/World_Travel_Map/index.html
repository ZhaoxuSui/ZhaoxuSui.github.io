The Travel Map of the World

Properties: A Map of the World for travel planning
Languages: English
Format: Digital, 14044 * 9934 pixels, JPG
Purpose: The Travel map of the world focus on not only general reference purposes, but specially designed for travel planning purposes. This map includes thousands of interesting travel destinations, attractions, and cities. It is the first detailed global scale travel map, and I am still improving it to make it better.
